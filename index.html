Aligning Items in the Container

  • justify-content
    - controls alignment of items on the main axis. 

  • align-items
    - controls alignment of items on the cross axis. 

  • align-content
    - sets how the browser distributes space between and around items along the cross axis. 

  • align-self
    - controls alignment of an individual item on the cross axis.
